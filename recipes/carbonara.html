Carbonara

Description

Tender sheets of pasta, a creamy ricotta cheese filling, and a rich meaty tomato sauce are all topped with more cheese and baked until browned and bubbly.

  • Cheese Filling
  • Meat
  • Sauce
  1. Boil pasta: Cook in a large pot of salted water
  2. Prepare meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic
  3. Combine cheese mixture: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl
  4. Layer & bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake
